Overview

Purpose

[Is there a holiday coming up that you forgot to buy gifts for? Look no further! My site is perfect for those that tend to wait until the last minute when buying gifts for others. With a few different options, a buyer will be able to pick between price ranges of what they would like to purchase and, because the selection is on the smaller side, choosing what to send is a walk in the park. The gift can then be sent directly to a person. As there is only a small selection of gifts to choose from, we will take suggestions as to what people would want for the upcoming holiday. So, when your memory gets the best of you, visit our site to pick out the best gift for anyone!]

Audience

[The target audience for this site are people with procrastination skills, or those who tend to put things off until the last minute, as well as people who have a hard time deciding what to get others. Waiting last minute is no problem for Gifts 4 Occasions as it gives a package with certain gifts in a set for people who are indecisive. It also allows those who wait last minute to order quickly and easily, meaning their package will be able to get to that special person quicker and easier.]

Content

Home page

[Here is Gifts 4 Occasions, the website that will help just about anybody who is indecisive, a little forgetful, and a bit of a procrastinator. You might be at this site is for three different reasons. One, you are indecisive in picking a gift for that special someone. Two, your memory may have gotten the best of you and the next upcoming holiday is getting closer without you having realized it. Three, you have procrastinated until the last minute to this upcoming holiday and do not have the time to think too hard for what to gift a special someone. Or a combination of all three! Either way, Gifts 4 Occasions is where you ended up and it is the perfect place for you!]

[Gifts 4 Occasions will allow you to choose from a variety of gift choices, all varying in prices and included items. Narrowing down these two options, both the price and the items included, will help your decision making much faster and easier, and who doesn't love a fast and easy process! The gifts update before each holiday, from Fourth of July and Halloween to Christmas and New Year's Eve! We even take recommendations for each holiday. So, start by clicking that "Shop Now" button and we will get you started with this fast and easy process!]

Images for the Home page

[Shop Now]

[Welcome to the shopping area of Gifts 4 Occasions! We try to make this part of the process fast and easy without too much of a hassle. Due to the nature of this company, we will only show gifts for the next two upcoming holidays. For example, Fourth of July gifts will be published alongside Halloween gifts. Then, once a holiday passes, that holiday will be up for another two weeks before the holiday proceeding the next holiday will be put up. We hope that by using this process, we will be giving enough time for those who were a little more forgetful about when the holiday was and needed more time to purchase a gift.]

[Now, down below, you will notice two separate categories. The first category is that of the soonest upcoming holiday. The second category is the next holiday proceeding the first labeled holiday. They are both labeled with which holiday it is, when the holiday is, and how long we will keep those certain items in stock for. Underneath each holiday are three different gift types. The first gift is the least expensive type, varying anywhere between $10 to $20, depending on the holiday and item that is being sold. The second gift is a little more expensive, with a price range between $25 and $50. The third gift type is the most expensive, with a price range between $50 and $100.Now, all that's left is to decide with price range fits the occasion and purchase it!]

[Contact Us]

[Contact us here! This page allows you to contact us here and is especially helpful in making recommendations for the upcoming holidays! Submit a form to us with what gift you might be hoping to see come to the site for purchasing during the next holiday. This will not only help you in possibly seeing your recommendation come to the site, but it also helps us know what people are looking for in terms of holiday gifts.]

[This is also a way for you to give us feedback on a couple different things. The first thing is our service. How did we do overall? Were you satisfied with our service? Did your special someone enjoy the gift they got? Another thing you could give us feedback on is how the gift selection for the current holiday is. This will be another way for us to gage our customer's satisfaction. There are so many things we would love to hear about and we would also love to hear about how we could do better! So don't hesitate to contact us! ]
